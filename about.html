Giuseppe Dicanio

At the core of my practice is an investigation into the relationship between individual elements and human perception, engaging with the dynamics of vision and interpretation. Everyday presences, objects, and diverse materials are recontextualized, altering their function and form while forging unexpected connections among themselves and with their surroundings.
