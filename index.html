Bio

Giuseppe Dicanio (2005, Pomarico) studies at Accademia di Belle Arti di Roma with Prof. Pierluigi Calignano. ​He spent the winter semester of 2025/2026 at the Hochschule für Bildende Künste Hamburg (HFBK), studying Time-Based Media with Prof. Jeanne Faust.​​

Statement

My research unfolds the perception of everyday reality, space, and time through photography, multimedia works and installations, focusing on specific everyday subjects,objects, and actions.
